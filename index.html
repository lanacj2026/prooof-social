New to DR/VE?

Start Here.

Not at all. Every class is designed to be approachable while still challenging. Our coaches offer guidance and modifications throughout class.
Classes can be booked through our website or the DR/VE app. We recommend booking ahead as some times fill early. Trouble booking? Call 405-593-3001 or email info@drivetrainingclub.com.
CYCLE requires cycling shoes with SPD or Delta clips — complimentary shoe rentals included for members and first-timers. TRAIN can be done in tennis shoes or socks. SCULPT is typically socks.
Bring water, grip socks or tennis shoes depending on format, and anything you'd normally bring to a workout. Towels are complimentary. Water + energy drinks are available for purchase in studio.
We recommend arriving 10–15 minutes before your first class so our team can check you in, show you the space, and get you comfortable before class starts.
All formats are beginner-friendly. Whether you're drawn to cardio, strength, or low-impact movement, our team will guide you to the best starting point.
