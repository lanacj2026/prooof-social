How it works

Simple as 1-2-3.

No complicated setup. Just join, post, and watch your streak grow.

01
Join a challenge
Browse hundreds of public challenges — fitness, wellness, finance, learning, and more. Or create your own and invite friends. Businesses, universities, and coaches host official challenges with real prizes and exclusive badges.
02
Post your proof
Upload a photo, screenshot, note, or check-in every day. Your proof goes into the challenge feed where your accountability partners can see it, react to it, and cheer you on. Day badge auto-stamps every post.
03
Build your streak
Watch your streak grow. Earn badges. Climb leaderboards. Get nudged on days you miss. Your profile becomes a public record of follow-through — proof over promises.

Badges

About PROOOF

We believe in proof over promises.

Most people don't fail their goals because they don't care. They fail because no one is watching — and when no one is watching, it's easy to let things slide.

PROOOF was built around one simple idea: your goals are more likely to happen when someone can see them. Not to embarrass you. Not to perform for your followers. Just a small circle of people who know what you're working on — and who will notice if you disappear.

We're not a productivity app. We're not a habit tracker. We're the group chat for getting your life together.
